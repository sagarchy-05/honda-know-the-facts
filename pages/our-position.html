Our Position
Maintaining competitive wages and benefits is nothing new for Honda, we’ve done it for more than 40 years. A union would not enhance the excellent employment experience of our associates or the job stability we provide.

OUR TEAM- ORIENTED APPROACH PRODUCES WORLD- CLASS PRODUCTS.

By promoting a positive and safe work environment and integrating ideas from associates throughout the company into our business, we have produced world- class products that exceed our customers’ expectations. Our team-oriented approach has helped us manage through challenging times, while maintaining our competitive edge in the marketplace. That’s why so many associates have made lifelong careers at Honda, and why we have multiple generations of “Honda families.”
Associates Decide
The issue of union representation is ultimately one for our associates to decide and, for more than four decades, Honda associates have spoken loudly and clearly in choosing to reject union outreach efforts.
We agree with our associates
that there is no need for outside
involvement to help us continue the
approach that has proven so
successful for the entire Honda team,
our communities and our customers.
