Your Voice Is Our Strength

  • The key to the success of our U.S. operations is “The Honda Way” – a culture based on teamwork, respect and open communication.
  • Over the past four decades, no automaker has been more successful than Honda in growing its production operations in America – now with 12 plants in seven states employing almost 23,000 associates in production-related roles.
  • Honda believes that having an outside party such as the UAW speaking on behalf of you and your fellow associates is not in the best interest of anyone. That’s not how we got here, and it’s not how we’ll get where we’re going.
  • But the ultimate decision regarding union representation should be made by associates.
At Honda, we empower our associates by communicating as one team. We invest in our people, value their ideas and ensure job security while delivering top-quality products.
